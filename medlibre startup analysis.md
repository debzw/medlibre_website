3. PRICING STRATEGY
Core Philosophy: Aggressive Disruptive Ladder
Enter cheap, prove value, upsell. The goal is to make the paid tier feel like "obviously worth it" compared to free, not to maximize per-user revenue in year 1.

Recommended Pricing Architecture
Tier 1 — Visitante (Guest) | FREE
5 questions/day
Interstitial ads every 3 questions
No account, no persistence
Purpose: SEO/SEA traffic conversion funnel
Tier 2 — Explorador (Free, Registered) | FREE
20 questions/day (reset midnight)
Lateral ads only
Full SRS, full statistics, directed study
Purpose: Habit formation. This is the core acquisition engine. Do NOT remove features — make it genuinely good.
Tier 3 — Premium | R$ 39.90/month or R$ 299/year
(R$ 24.92/month billed annually = 37.5% discount)

Unlimited questions/day
Zero ads
PDF export (up to 20/day)
Priority email support
Performance heatmaps + weak-area detection
"Modo Simulado" (timed exam simulation)
Purpose: Revenue core; annual plan is the conversion target
Tier 4 — Premium+ / Intensivo | R$ 499/year (future, 6-12 months out)
Everything in Premium
Monthly group mentorship session (live Q&A with specialist)
"Cronograma Adaptativo" (auto-generated study plan based on exam date + current SRS state)
Early access to new question sets
Purpose: Upsell for high-commitment students; increases LTV without increasing COGS much
